About This System

Thyroid Nodule Detection Pipeline

This system uses a Convolutional Neural Network (CNN) trained on thyroid ultrasound images to classify nodules as Benign or Malignant. Additionally, a pre-trained EfficientNet model from HuggingFace is available for rapid inference. Data augmentation techniques are applied during training for robust generalization.
